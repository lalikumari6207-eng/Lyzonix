Our Journey

From curious learners
to a growing group of brands

2017

The Spark

Our journey began when we first discovered the world of IT — curious learners with a big vision to do something meaningful with technology.

2018–23

Building in the Dark

Without an official company, we connected with local people, explored opportunities, and kept learning — even when doubters said it wouldn't work. Consistency was our strategy.

2024

First Client. Real Beginning.

We landed our first paying client — a milestone that turned years of preparation into a professional journey. The company became real.

2024–26

Growing Fast

12+ clients. 50+ projects. Multiple industries. From healthcare to education, NGOs to startups — we've delivered results that matter.

Now

Becoming a Group

What started as one IT company has grown into Lyzonix Group — now home to Lyzonix Technologies, Sanjhex, and Lali's Homie.
