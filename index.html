About Us

Lyzonix Technologies is a modern IT solutions company dedicated to helping businesses grow faster and smarter. We provide a complete range of services including IT solutions, web & app development and data analytics.

Our goal is simple — to make business operations easy by combining technology and human resource expertise. Whether you are a startup or an established company, we help you build, manage, and scale your team and digital presence.

We specialize in:

  • ✔ IT Solutions & Development
  • ✔ Data & Business Analytics
  • ✔ Cloud Solutions

Our Story

The idea of Lyzonix Technologies began back in 2017, when we first got introduced to the world of IT. At that time, we were just curious learners with a vision to do something meaningful in technology.

Along with our founder and co-founder, we decided to give it a try — even without officially starting a company. We began by connecting with local people, discussing ideas, and exploring opportunities. In the beginning, many people doubted us and thought it wouldn’t work.

Despite the challenges, we stayed consistent, kept learning, and continued improving our skills in development, analytics, and real-world problem solving.

In 2024, we achieved a major milestone by getting our first client. That moment marked the real beginning of our professional journey.

From 2024 to 2026, we have successfully worked with 12+ clients and delivered 50+ projects across different domains.

Today, Lyzonix Technologies stands as a growing IT solutions company, committed to helping businesses build, grow, and succeed in the digital world.
